Benefits: Nutrient-rich: High in vitamins, minerals, healthy fats, and protein. Heart & bone health: Help lower cholesterol and support bone strength. Blood sugar & weight control: Low glycemic index and promote satiety. Downsides: High in calories: Can lead to weight gain if overconsumed. Allergies: Can cause reactions in some people. Digestive issues: May cause bloating in excess. Other risks: May contribute to kidney stones, interfere with mineral absorption, and pose choking hazards for young children.
